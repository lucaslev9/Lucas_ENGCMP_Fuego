Field or Career

At its core, computer science is the study of problems, problem-solving, and the solutions to these problems. From developing an app to track personal fitness, to writing an algorithm to predict natural disasters, computer science identifies a problem and seeks to solve it. Whether it be resolving the smallest or the largest issue, computer science is inherently improving the world. I think I decided on computer science largely due to my exposure to and fascination with computers and technology at a young age. I was attending tech camps by the time I was in middle school, and I guess I just knew that it was what I wanted to do.
